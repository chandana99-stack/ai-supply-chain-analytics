# 🚀 AI-Powered Supply Chain Analytics Platform

## 📌 Project Overview
An end-to-end supply chain analytics solution built using 
Python, SQL, Machine Learning, and AI to deliver demand 
forecasting, anomaly detection, and business insights 
from 180,519 real supply chain orders.

## 🎯 Business Problem
57% of supply chain deliveries are late, costing businesses 
millions annually. This project builds an intelligent 
analytics platform that:
- 📦 Analyzes 180,519 real supply chain orders
- 🔮 Forecasts product demand using Machine Learning
- 🚨 Automatically detects supply chain anomalies using AI
- 📊 Visualizes KPIs through interactive charts

## 💡 Key Business Insights Found
- **57.3%** of all deliveries are late
- **Product Price** is the #1 driver of demand
- **9,024** anomalous orders detected automatically by AI
- **Western Europe** is the top sales region ($5.89M)
- **Field & Stream Sportsman Gun Safe** is the #1 product

## 📊 Notebooks Summary

### 📓 01 - Data Cleaning & EDA
- Loaded and cleaned 180,519 rows of supply chain data
- Fixed missing values and engineered new features
- Created delivery delay and late delivery columns
- Visualized top products, regions, and delivery performance

### 🤖 02 - ML Demand Forecasting
- Built Random Forest model to predict product demand
- Trained on 144,415 samples, tested on 36,104 samples
- Average prediction error of only 0.77 units
- Identified Product Price as the top demand driver

### 🚨 03 - AI Anomaly Detection
- Built Isolation Forest AI model
- Automatically detected 9,024 suspicious orders
- No manual checking required — fully automated
- Visualized anomalies by delivery delay and profit ratio
